Amalfi

After seeing Florence and Venice which included much walking and sight seeing, Robyn and I knew we had to experience a different side of Italy...a more relaxing side. Income Amalfi!, we trained to Salerno and then ferried to Amalfi. From first sight we knew we were in for a treat of relaxation. Our experience consisted of minimal sight seeing and maximum stress free beach bumming, which was fantastic. Our stay was 2 minutes from the beach, perched up right next to the 9th century cathedral. We knew since we were in Italy there was going to be a church somewhere! Amalfi presented to us how the Italians live during holiday, in the mediterranean...
